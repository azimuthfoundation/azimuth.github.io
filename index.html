Incentives and Monetization Tools

Engage-to-Earn

Users monetization and other lucrative opportunities are achieved by engagement mining, in which every user's sharing, posting, commenting and liking are directly rewarded.

Sponsored NFTs

Every post is an NFT, allowing anyone across the network to purchase its ad space real-estate. The revenue generated from each post is shared between the post owner(s) and the post publisher.

User Rewards

Every user/creator profile can keep a certain percentage of the minted tokens as a "Founder Reward". Moreover, users are rewarded for their legacy social media clout.

Invest and Boost

Support other creators by investing in their creator tokens and help them gain engagement by leveraging your own community and get returns for their success.
